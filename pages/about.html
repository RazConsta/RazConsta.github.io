About me

I am a rising sophomore at University of Washington Tacoma pursuing a BS in Computer Science and Systems and my current goal is to secure a internship for summer 2021 or summer 2022. I enjoy programming, math and tech. My history with programming goes back to fifth grade, when I enrolled in my school's programming club, where we learned Pascal. However, after a couple of months, the club faced some funding issues and was disbanded. After a long break, I resumed programming in 9th grade through my high school's C++ classes. Following that, AP Computer Science in 12th grade introduced me to Java and the beauty of object-oriented programming. From there, it was pretty clear to me that I should follow a computer science path in college, and I am currently doing so and loving every moment of it.

When I am not studying, some of my hobbies include gaming, going to the movies and biking.
